About Me

Who am I?

My name is Jack Leahey. I am currently on the path to graduate from Rochester Institute of Technology in May of 2023 with a Bachelors of Science degree in Computing Security with a minor in Mathematics. I grew up in Warwick, NY and graduated Warwick Valley High School in 2018. Aside from working at my current place of employment and running a website developing business, I am a really big baseball fan. I have been to over 15 MLB parks across the country with the goal of eventually attending a game at all 30 MLB teams ballparks (Go Yankees!). You can also catch me watching college sports games or professional basketball and football games on the television. Aside from sports and computers, I also sometimes play the guitar in my free time and like to cook.



Why Computers?

As a typical high school student, I was unsure of what field I wanted to pursue going into college. I had educated myself on writing HTML and CSS scripting languages, and decided to go into computer science. After some course work in computer science, I quickly realized that this isn't my fieldand switched to computing security. Since then, I have enjoyed studying networking topics including network routing and switching, Windows Active Directory topics, Linux Operating Systems, among others, and cyber security topics including being exposed to different attacks in a controlled environment, experience with certain tools, etc. Over the years, I have kept up my passion for HTML/CSS scripting that I have learned in high school by making websites like this one.
